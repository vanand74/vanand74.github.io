Biography
Vic Anand joined Texas State University in the summer of 2025. Previously, he worked at the Gies College of Business at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign from 2017 - 2025, and Goizueta Business School of Emory University from 2012 - 2017. He completed his PhD in Accounting at Cornell University. He also holds an MBA from Carnegie Mellon and an SB in mechanical engineering from MIT. Anand's professional experience includes serving as a senior analyst with Science Applications International Corp., a senior consultant with Deloitte Consulting, and a financial analyst and manufacturing engineer with Ford Motor Company.

Anand's research is in managerial accounting and focuses on the use of goals and targets in accounting settings, and cost systems.
